Grants ($13.2M CAD)

Our research group is consistently supported by grants from leading national and international funding institutions. These grants acknowledge the significance of our work and empower us to pursue groundbreaking discoveries in Quantum Communications and Quantum Computing, Wireless Communications and Networks, Physical layer security and privacy, Machine Learning and Realtime Optimisation, Disaster Management, Smart Agriculture, Healthcare, Air-Quality, Finance, Climate Change, and Pharmacy.

  • 2024 – 2026: “Edge Techniques for Video Delivery System in 6G Networks: Analysis, Design, and Optimisation”, National Foundation for Science &Technology Development; $70,000 CAD (Co-I)
  • 2024-2028: “Research on Sensing, Actuation, Communication, and Intelligent Signal Processing Infrastructure for Ambient Intelligence”, Japan Science and Technology Agency (JST), JPY 500M = $5,000,000 CAD (Co-I)
  • 2023-2024: “Quantum Machine Learning Meets 6G: Quantum Interference Alignment and 6G Security,” Taiwan National Science and Technology Council (NSTC), Engineering technology Track, $6,000 CAD (PI)
  • 2023-2031: “Next Generation Communications Technology”, Canada Natural Sciences and Engineering Research Council (NSERC), $8,000,000 CAD (PI)
